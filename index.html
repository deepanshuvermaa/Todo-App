      HOW IT WORKS

      1

      Connect Your Google Account

      Go to Settings and click "Connect Google Account". The app will create a personal spreadsheet in your Google Drive.

      2

      Add Your Daily Tasks

      Type tasks in the input field and press Enter. Tasks appear instantly and sync to your Google Sheet.

      3

      Check Off Completed Tasks

      Click the checkbox when you complete a task. It moves to the "Completed" section with a strikethrough.

      4

      Track Your Progress

      View your daily completion percentage and procrastination metrics in the overview panel.

      5

      Automatic Task Rollover

      At midnight, any unfinished tasks automatically move to the next day. No task gets forgotten!

      DATA STORAGE

      Your data is stored in two places:

      • Browser Storage: Tasks are saved locally for instant access and offline use
      • Google Sheets: When connected, tasks sync to your personal Google Sheet for backup and access from anywhere

      Privacy: Your tasks are never stored on our servers. Everything stays in your browser and your Google Drive.
